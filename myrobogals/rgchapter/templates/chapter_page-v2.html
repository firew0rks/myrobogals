Our Purpose

We at Robogals Monash believe that more young women can pursue a career Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics and try to inspire them to do so. We do this by running robotics workshops for Year 3 to 12 students at schools and at Monash University. Our Lego Mindstorms EV3 robotics workshops endeavour to provide the students with an understanding of what engineering entails through presentations and set interactive programming tasks designed to encourage problem-solving and critical-thinking skills. Our volunteers are equipped with professional development opportunities that have a focus on strengthening communication and leadership skills and have a chance to apply for committee, regional and global roles within the organisation.
