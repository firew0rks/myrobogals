About

Robogals Monash is a not-for-profit, student-run organisation that aims to encourage more young women to pursue Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics career opportunities, with a particular focus on engineering. We do this by running robotics workshops for Year 3 to 12 students at schools and at Monash University. Our Lego Mindstorms EV3 robotics workshops endeavour to provide the students with an understanding of what engineering entails through presentations and set interactive programming tasks designed to encourage problem-solving and critical-thinking skills. Our volunteers are equipped with professional development opportunities that have a focus on strengthening communication and leadership skills and have a chance to apply for committee, regional and global roles within the organisation.
